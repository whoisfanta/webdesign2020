Hello, Future JoBro.

This entire page is a small dedication to JoJo's Bizarre Adventure.

"But programming person,
what the hell is a JoJo's Bizarre Adventure?"

In its most basic form, JoJo's is a japanese manga and animated series
that depicts the adventures of the Joestar family. Across several generations,
they are destined to fight and defeat a greater evil, in order to save humanity.
In parts 1 and 2, characters fight using Hamon, and in every other part they fight through the use of Stands.
It was and still is being written by mangaka Hirohiko Araki.

The animated series is currently divided in 5 seasons, commonly known as "parts". Each part features a new main character - a new JoJo.

Each part also features a distinct villain, each with their own looks, powers and motivations for evil-doing.

But under the microscope, it's much more than that. It has everything you could ask for.

Amazing writing, plot and acting.

Superb animation and soundtrack
(which you're hopefully hearing right now),
which lead to incredible fighting scenes.

But where it shines most
is in not taking itself too seriously...

...unless the situation calls for it.

Above all, every part tells a story of friendship and comradery.

JoJo's is part of a piece of culture that I was never very in touch with.
But thanks to it, I've made what I hope to be lifelong friendships,
all brought together by something that has a little bit of everything - for everyone.
